THE JOKER TERRORIZES GOTHAM CITY

The most dangerous madman on the face of the Earth and Batman's arch-nemesis, The Joker is on the loose, spreading fear and panic throughout the streets! His diabolical arsenal of weapons seem at first like harmless practical jokes, but in reality are tools of mayhem and destruction. Be on the lookout for his joy-buzzer, which delivers a lethal electric charge, and his trick flower, which squirts a powerful acid. The most dangerous item in his arsenal is the secret weapon, Smylex, a chemical which gives its victims a permanent, hideous grin!
